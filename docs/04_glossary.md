## Camino
A Camino is a user-defined signal path that carries musical information through MIOS.

A Camino may transport audio, MIDI, or expressive control signals independently or in combination, allowing musicians to design the behavior of their instrument through flexible routing.

Multiple Caminos can exist independently or converge to create new musical interactions.

## Junta

A Junta is a user-defined collection of related modules that together perform a single musical role.

Juntas simplify complex signal graphs by allowing multiple modules to be viewed and managed as a single musical unit.

Examples include:

• Guitar Junta
• Synth Junta
• Looper Junta
• Ambient Texture Junta
